   - Competitive landscape analysis and gap identification
   - Technical SEO audit and site health assessment
   - Customer journey mapping and pain point analysis

2. **Strategy Roadmap** — prioritized experiments and channel plan.
   - Channel prioritization based on business objectives
   - Budget allocation and resource planning

   - KPI framework and success metrics definition

3. **Creative Production** — briefs, assets, and campaign build.
   - Creative brief development and asset production

4. **Launch & Optimize** — monitor, iterate, and scale winners.

   - Real-time performance tracking and optimization

5. **Scale & Automate** — bake automation into repeatable workflows.
   - Marketing automation implementation and optimization
   - Reporting dashboard setup and executive briefings
   - Process documentation and team training
   - Long-term growth strategy and roadmap planning
