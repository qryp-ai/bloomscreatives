## Process
1. **Creative Brief & Goals** — target, distribution, and KPI.
   - Deep dive into brand objectives and campaign goals
   - Target audience analysis and platform selection
   - Content performance metrics and success criteria definition
   - Budget allocation and timeline establishment
   - Creative constraints and brand guidelines review

2. **Pre‑production** — scripts, shot lists, scheduling.
   - Creative concept development and storyboarding
   - Script writing and content planning
   - Location scouting and talent casting
   - Equipment planning and technical requirements
   - Production scheduling and logistics coordination

3. **Production** — on-location or studio shoots.
   - Professional filming and photography sessions
   - Live event coverage and real-time production
   - Voice-over recording and audio capture
   - Motion graphics creation and animation production
   - Quality control and on-set creative direction

4. **Post‑production** — edits, colour, sound mix.
   - Video editing and assembly of final cuts
   - Color grading and visual enhancement
   - Audio mixing and sound design integration
   - Motion graphics compositing and visual effects
   - Multi-format rendering and platform optimization

5. **Distribution Support** — crop variants, captions and platform specs.
   - Content adaptation for different social media platforms
   - Automated caption generation and subtitle creation
   - Asset organization and digital asset management setup
   - Performance tracking and analytics integration
   - Content repurposing strategy and implementation
